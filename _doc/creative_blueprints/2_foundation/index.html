The Problem

Fashion is
scattered everywhere.

  • The same item is priced differently across ASOS, Farfetch, Zalando, and brand sites — shoppers overpay without knowing it.

  • Exclusive drops are platform-specific — by the time they're found manually, they're already sold out.

  • 15–30 minutes of tab-switching per item is the norm — every serious fashion shopper knows the pain.

The Solution

One search.
Every platform.
Best price, instantly.

  • Search by item, brand, or description — the app queries all major fashion platforms simultaneously.

  • Platform-exclusive items surface automatically at the top of results — flagged before they sell out.

  • Best price highlighted with a direct buy link — from discovery to checkout in seconds, not minutes.
