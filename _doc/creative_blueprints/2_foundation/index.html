Fashion fragmentation across platforms

The Problem

Fashion shoppers today face a fragmented experience. The same dress might be available on 5 different platforms at wildly different prices. Finding exclusive items and comparing costs requires manual searching across countless sites, eating up time and leading to overspending.

The Solution

Fashion items are scattered across dozens of platforms—retailers, marketplaces, and boutiques—making it nearly impossible to compare prices, availability, and exclusivity in one place. Shoppers waste time switching between tabs, miss exclusive deals, and often overpay for items available cheaper elsewhere.

Target Audience

Fashion-conscious shoppers who value both style and savings

Personal Motivation

Empowering shoppers to make smarter fashion choices by removing friction from price discovery
