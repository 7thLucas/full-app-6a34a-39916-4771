# Faseyeon — Cross-Platform Fashion Price Comparison

## Brand
- **Name:** Faseyeon
- **Tagline:** Every piece. Every price. One search.
- **Tone:** Premium, editorial, fashion-forward. Fast and frictionless.

## Users
- Fashion shoppers seeking the best deal
- Exclusive drop hunters tracking limited releases
- Resellers monitoring price spreads
- Personal stylists sourcing items for clients

## Core Concept
Faseyeon lets users search any fashion item by name and instantly see prices compared across all major platforms in one clean view. No tab-switching, no guesswork — just the best price with a direct buy link.

## Platforms Covered
ASOS, Farfetch, Zalando, Net-a-Porter, Zara, H&M, and brand direct sites.

## Key Capabilities
- **Instant cross-platform search** — type a product name, see all prices side by side
- **Exclusive drops** — limited-edition and drop items automatically flagged and surfaced at the top of results
- **Best price highlight** — lowest price is visually distinguished with a direct buy link
- **Direct buy links** — each result links out to the retailer product page

## Strategic Principles
- Speed and frictionlessness above all else
- Editorial confidence — the UI should feel like a fashion magazine, not a coupon site
- Surface signal over noise: drops and deals rise to the top automatically
- Trust through transparency: show platform name, price, and availability clearly
